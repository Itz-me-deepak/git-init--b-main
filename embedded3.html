Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS)

Course Title: Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS)

Introduction

This course introduces you to RTOS principles and how real-time operating systems manage multiple tasks, ensure timing accuracy, and control resource access in embedded systems.

Importance

RTOS enables predictability and concurrency — essential for time-critical applications like robotics, automotive ECUs, and industrial controllers.

Why Choose This Course?

  • Understand task creation, priority-based scheduling, and preemption.
  • Learn synchronization with semaphores, queues, and mutexes.
  • Explore FreeRTOS, Zephyr, and other popular RTOS platforms.
  • Manage CPU time, memory, and inter-process communication efficiently.

Outcomes & Advantages

  • Design real-time embedded systems with multitasking capability.
  • Handle inter-task communication and resource sharing.
  • Deploy scalable, reliable, and time-deterministic applications.
  • Prepares you for advanced robotics and automation roles.

Course Duration

5 weeks

Includes

RTOS labs, task scheduling simulations, real-time interrupt handling, and FreeRTOS-based mini project.
