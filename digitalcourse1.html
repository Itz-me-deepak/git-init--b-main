Digital VLSI Design

Course Title: Digital VLSI Design

Introduction

Digital VLSI (Very Large Scale Integration) Design is the foundation of modern digital electronics. This course focuses on the design, simulation, and verification of digital integrated circuits using HDL (Hardware Description Languages) and logic synthesis tools.

Importance

From microprocessors to memory chips, all digital systems rely on VLSI design. Understanding how to model and optimize logic circuits is crucial for building efficient and scalable digital systems.

Why Choose This Course?

  • Learn Verilog/SystemVerilog for RTL design and simulation.
  • Understand combinational and sequential logic, FSMs, datapaths, and control units.
  • Gain hands-on experience with EDA tools like ModelSim, Vivado, or Synopsys VCS.
  • Foundation for careers in digital design, ASIC/FPGA development, and embedded hardware.

Outcomes & Advantages

  • Write synthesizable RTL for digital systems.
  • Perform functional and timing simulation.
  • Build digital subsystems like ALUs, multiplexers, memory blocks, etc.
  • Prepares you for roles in semiconductor, embedded, and automation industries.

Mode
