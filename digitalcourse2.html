Advanced Digital VLSI Design

Course Title: Advanced Digital VLSI Design

Introduction

This course dives deeper into the architectural, optimization, and verification aspects of digital IC design. It focuses on advanced RTL coding, low-power design, pipelining, design-for-testability (DFT), and formal verification concepts.

Importance

As chip complexity increases, advanced knowledge of design methodologies, power-aware coding, and verification strategies becomes essential to meet modern silicon demands.

Why Choose This Course?

  • Go beyond basic RTL – learn pipelining, parallelism, clock gating, and synthesis optimization.
  • Understand DFT techniques like scan insertion, ATPG, and boundary scan.
  • Explore formal verification and assertion-based design.
  • Industry-relevant skills for advanced ASIC design, FPGA prototyping, and SoC development.

Outcomes & Advantages

  • Design complex digital blocks with low-power and high-speed goals.
  • Apply DFT methodologies for testable designs.
  • Gain exposure to advanced verification strategies.
  • Boost your readiness for top roles in VLSI, SoC design, and chip verification.

Duration

8 weeks

Mode

Online / Offline

Includes

Hands-on labs, synthesis optimization tasks, and a DFT-aware project.
