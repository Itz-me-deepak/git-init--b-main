About Zepto Logic Technologies

Empowering Future Innovators

We are a forward-thinking semiconductor solutions company focused on building skilled talent and delivering high-quality VLSI and embedded system services. By offering strong exposure to industry practices and tools, we bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application, driving innovation through hands-on experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower future semiconductor professionals and support organizations through practical, application-driven training and services. We aim to build a strong talent ecosystem skilled in VLSI, embedded systems, and chip design. These professionals will be equipped to lead with confidence in a fast-evolving tech landscape.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a global hub for semiconductor excellence, where talent meets opportunity, innovation drives progress, and education powers long-term growth. We are committed to shaping the industry through workforce development, cutting-edge technology, and meaningful collaboration.
